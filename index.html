ABOUT US

When sisters Hari Priya and Darshna​ Patel left their salon in India after twelve years in the beauty industry, they decided to bring their experience and traditional Indian practices across the seas to Maryland. To this day, we still hold true to our roots, providing services such as sari wrapping, henna and herbal facials, and we'd just love to have you as a part of our story. For more information on our services, check our Services page.
